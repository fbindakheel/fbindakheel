📖 Poker Game Help & Rules

Basic Gameplay

This is a Texas Hold'em Poker game. Each player is dealt two private cards (hole cards). Five community cards are dealt face-up on the 'board'. Players use their hole cards along with the community cards to make the best possible five-card poker hand.

Betting Rounds

  • Pre-flop: After hole cards are dealt.
  • Flop: After the first three community cards are dealt.
  • Turn: After the fourth community card is dealt.
  • River: After the fifth community card is dealt.

In each round, players can Check, Bet, Call, Raise, or Fold.
